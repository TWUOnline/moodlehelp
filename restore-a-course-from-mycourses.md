<div class="clarify-article">
<h2 class="clarify-article-title">Restore a Course in Moodle</h2>

<div class="clarify-steps-container">
<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-1">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">From your empty course site in Moodle, click &#39;Restore&#39; in the &#39;Administration&#39; block.</h3>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="580" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/f93p2m/from-your-empty-course-site-in-moodle--click--restore--in-the--administration--block.png?1499362776" width="284" /></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-2">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">Drag the zipped file (this file must end in .zip) to the area indicated in Moodle, then click &#39;Restore&#39;.</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-instructions">
<p>Alternately, you can click &#39;Choose a file&#39; to use the file picker.</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="415" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/f93p2m/drag-the-zipped-file--this-file-must-end-in-zip--to-the-area-indicated-in-moodle--then-click--restor.png?1499362776" width="739" /></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-3">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">You will be warned that the file you have chosen is not a standard Moodle backup file. You should ignore this warning and click &#39;Continue&#39;.</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="292" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/f93p2m/you-will-be-warned-that-the-file-you-have-chosen-is-not-a-standard-moodle-backup-file-you-should-ign.png?1499362778" width="742" /></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-4">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">Under the heading &#39;Restore into this course&#39;, choose &#39;Delete the contents...&#39;, then click &#39;Continue&#39;.</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="239" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/f93p2m/under-the-heading--restore-into-this-course---choose--delete-the-contents---then-click--continue-.png?1499362778" width="399" /></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-5">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">Use the checkboxes to control what you restore. Scroll to the bottom and click &#39;Next&#39;.</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="733" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/f93p2m/use-the-checkboxes-to-control-what-you-restore-scroll-to-the-bottom-and-click--next-.png?1499362779" width="724" /></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-6">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">Set the Course start date to the first Monday of September.</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-instructions">
<p>Don&#39;t change any of the other settings.</p>
</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="233" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/f93p2m/set-the-course-start-date-to-the-first-monday-of-september.png?1499362779" width="658" /></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-7">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">Scroll to the bottom of the page and click &#39;Next&#39;.</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="94" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/f93p2m/scroll-to-the-bottom-of-the-page-and-click--next-.png?1499362780" width="713" /></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-8">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">Scroll to the bottom of the page and click &#39;Perform restore&#39;.</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="92" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/f93p2m/scroll-to-the-bottom-of-the-page-and-click--perform-restore-.png?1499362781" width="711" /></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-9">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">Click &#39;Continue&#39; to see your newly restored course.</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="153" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/f93p2m/click--continue--to-see-your-newly-restored-course.png?1499362782" width="730" /></div>
</div>
</div>
