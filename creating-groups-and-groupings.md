<h2 class="clarify-article-title">Creating Groups and Groupings</h2>

<div class="clarify-article-description">
<p>Groups can be a useful tool in Moodle, and can have several functions; It can be used to give students a platform in the forums to interact with one another in a smaller group within the class, or they can be used to allow separate drop boxes for assignments designated to specific groups.</p>

<p>To learn how to create Groups, see the steps below.</p>
</div>

<div class="clarify-steps-container">
<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-1">
<hr />
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">Part 1: Creating a Group</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-instructions">
<p>Login to the course in which you would like to create a new group.</p>

<p>Click the &quot;Users&quot; link on the Administration block, and then select &quot;Groups&quot; from the drop down menu.</p>
</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="553" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/4xetrg/part-1--creating-a-group.png?1495748710" width="308" /></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-2">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">Next,</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-instructions">
<p>Once you are redirected to the group creation page, click the <strong>&quot;Create Group&quot;</strong> button located near the bottom of the page.</p>

<p>Supply a &quot;Group Name&quot; (e.g Group 1).</p>

<p>Fill in &quot;Group Description&quot; and add a group picture (optional).</p>

<p>Click &quot;Save Changes&quot; at the bottom of the page.</p>

<p>After you have saved your changes, you will be directed back to the group creation page. Repeat the above steps to create more groups.</p>
</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="1696" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/4xetrg/next-.png?1495748711" width="923" /></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-3">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">&nbsp;</h3>

<h3 class="clarify-step-title">Part 2: Adding Users to a Group</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-instructions">
<p>Out of the groups you have now created, select a Group name from the list on the left hand side of the page.</p>

<p>Click &quot;Add/remove users&quot; button under the box on the right hand side of the page.</p>
</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="778" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/4xetrg/part-2--adding-users-to-a-group.png?1495748712" width="920" /></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-5">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">Next,</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-instructions">
<p>Select a name from the &quot;<strong>Potential Members</strong>&quot; list on the right side of the page, and click on <strong>&quot;Add&quot;.</strong></p>

<p>Continue to <strong>&quot;Add&quot;</strong> all of the students that you wish to assign to that particular group.</p>

<p>Once you are finished assigning group members to that particular group, click the &quot;<strong>Back to Groups</strong>&quot; button at the bottom of the page.</p>

<p><strong>Repeat the steps above to add members to the remaining groups.</strong></p>
</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="784" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/4xetrg/next-1.png?1495748713" width="925" /></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-6">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">&nbsp;</h3>

<h3 class="clarify-step-title">Part 3: Creating &quot;Groupings&quot;</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-instructions">
<p>Once &quot;Groups&quot; have been created and students have been assigned, you have the further options of creating &quot;Groupings.&quot;</p>

<p>A grouping is a collection of groups within a course. Using groupings allows you to direct tasks at one or more groups in your course, so that they can work together on the tasks.</p>

<p>In the group creation page, find the menu bar near the top of the page and click on the orange link that reads &quot;Groupings.&quot;</p>

<p>Click the &quot;Create grouping&quot; button.</p>
</div>

<div class="clarify-step-image-wrapper">
<div class="clarify-step-image-container"><img alt="" class="clarify-step-image" height="329" src="http://media.screensteps.me/e-support/4xetrg/part-3--creating--groupings-.png?1495748714" width="825" /></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="clarify-clear">&nbsp;</div>

<div class="clarify-step-container" id="clarify-step-8">
<h3 class="clarify-step-title">&nbsp;</h3>

<h3 class="clarify-step-title">Next steps,</h3>

<div class="clarify-step-instructions">
<p>Provide a name for the &quot;Grouping&quot; (this can be the same as the group name).</p>

<p>Create as many Groupings as needed,.</p>

<p>Next, the right &quot;Group&quot; must be associated with the corresponding &quot;Grouping.&quot; To do this, find the icon that looks like two people on the far right hand side of the page, underneath the &quot;Edit&quot; heading of the first &quot;Grouping.&quot; Select the appropriate group name from the &quot;Potential members&quot; list on the right hand side, and click &quot;Add.&quot;</p>

<p>Click &quot;Back to Groupings&quot; when you have finished, and repeat the previous step for the remaining &quot;Groupings.&quot;</p>
</div>
